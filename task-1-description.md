# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic

## Tool Used
Canva (canva.com)

## What the Infographic Shows

The infographic is titled **"Be Digitally Smart – A Guide for Students"** and covers three main topics:

---

### 1. What is Digital Literacy?
- The ability to find, evaluate, and use information from digital sources
- Knowing how to use digital tools safely and effectively
- Understanding your rights and responsibilities online

### 2. Safe Internet Practices
- Always use strong, unique passwords for each account
- Do not share personal information like phone number or address with strangers online
- Think before you click on unknown links or attachments
- Use two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Log out from shared or public devices

### 3. Professional Online Presence
- Keep your social media profiles clean and professional
- Use platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn to showcase your work
- What you post online stays there — even if you delete it later
- Your digital footprint reflects who you are to future employers

---

## Design Choices
- Colour scheme: Blue and white (clean, professional look)
- Icons used for each section to make it visually appealing
- Short bullet points for easy reading
- Designed in a single-column layout for better readability

---

## Reflection
Creating this infographic was a good experience. The most challenging part was fitting all the important information into a single page without making it look cluttered. I ended up cutting a lot of points and keeping only the most important ones. It was interesting to learn how visual design can communicate information faster than plain text.
